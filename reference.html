Who We Are

A Federation Built on Complementary Strengths

DCRF is a joint initiative of TCU Impact Foundation (TCUIF) and DiCAF. TCUIF brings research capacity and the media and corporate reach of TheCSRUniverse; DiCAF brings deep technical expertise in disaster resilience and climate action.

India faces disasters of growing frequency and complexity — floods, cyclones, heatwaves, earthquakes and climate-induced events that collectively cost the economy over ₹3 lakh crore annually. DCRF was established on 4 June 2026 to bridge the gaps in corporate engagement, media coverage and institutional advocacy.

TCU Impact Foundation (TCUIF)

A research and social impact advisory platform covering Sustainability, CSR, ESG and SDGs. Operates flagship platforms CASCA and SICA. Registered under Section 8, Companies Act 2013. CIN: U85500UP2024NPL198637.

DiCAF — Disaster & Climate Action Federation

A recognised Think Tank in Disaster Resilience and Climate Action, with expertise in Risk Assessment, Climate Finance, Geospatial Technology, R&D and Standards. Owner of disastersnews.com. CIN: U88900DL2024NPL425948.
